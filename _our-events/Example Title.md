                            <div class="content"><div style="margin-bottom: 3rem;"><h3 id="frequently-asked-questions"><strong>Frequently Asked Questions</strong></h3>

<div class="col is-large bp-accordion-header padding has-icons-right field has-addons is-marginless">
						<div class="col is-expanded is-fullwidth is-paddingless">
							<h5 class="has-text-grey-dark is-marginless">
								<b style="cursor:default;">1.  How would adopting the National Workplace Learning Framework help organisations?</b>
							</h5>
						</div>
						<span class="sgds-icon sgds-icon-plus is-size-4 bp-accordion-button"></span>
					</div><div id="accordion-body-0" class="col padding bp-accordion-body">
						<div class="bp-container is-full padding--top--lg padding--bottom" style="width: 100%">
<p>The <a href="/framework/national-workplace-learning-framework/">National Workplace Learning Framework</a> provides a pathway for organisations to build up a sustainable organisation-wide workplace learning eco-system that forms the foundation of a learning organisation.</p>

<p>By adopting the framework, organisations can align capability development and business needs and establish best-in-class workplace learning practices.</p>

</div>
					</div><div class="col is-large bp-accordion-header padding has-icons-right field has-addons is-marginless">
						<div class="col is-expanded is-fullwidth is-paddingless">
							<h5 class="has-text-grey-dark is-marginless">
								<b style="cursor:default;">2.  What constitutes the National Workplace Learning Framework?</b>
							</h5>
						</div>
						<span class="sgds-icon sgds-icon-plus is-size-4 bp-accordion-button"></span>
					</div><div id="accordion-body-1" class="col padding bp-accordion-body">
						<div class="bp-container is-full padding--top--lg padding--bottom" style="width: 100%">
<p>The <a href="/framework/national-workplace-learning-framework/">National Workplace Learning Framework</a> consists of six components, described briefly below:</p>

<p><a href="/framework/strategy/">1. Strategy</a><br>
The organisation is aware of the competencies it needs in order to achieve its goals and be successful. The essential competencies for the organisation are identified and validated and measures are defined.</p>

<p><a href="/framework/leadership/">2. Leadership</a><br>
The organisation establishes policies and practices that foster a learning-friendly culture. Leaders consistently communicate that learning is essential for the organisation’s growth and success in the medium and long term. Promoting continuous learning at work is recognised as an essential leadership task.</p>

<p><a href="/framework/planning/">3. Planning</a><br>
The organisation organises workplace learning activities systematically and purposefully. It supports the growth and development of its employees, trainers and leaders. The quality of workplace learning is continuously improved to suit current and future business needs.</p>

<p><a href="/framework/training-needs-analysis/">4.Training Needs Analysis</a><br>
The organisation regularly conducts its own workplace training needs analysis. It has a good grasp of its overall manpower skills requirements and the job specific training and workplace learning solutions needed.</p>

<p><a href="/framework/environment/">5. Environment</a><br>
The organisation supports learning at work and creates opportunities for learning at work to happen for a diversity of employee profiles. A positive environment for workplace learning is evident and suitable approaches for workplace learning are used for diverse employees’ needs and profiles.</p>

<p><a href="/framework/implementation-&amp;-processes/">6. Implementation &amp; Processes</a><br>
The organisation implements workplace learning activities, in a systematic and deliberate manner, based on employee learning profiles and skills needs. Appropriate learning delivery solutions are selected, and suitable instructional methods and job aids are used.</p>

</div>
					</div><div class="col is-large bp-accordion-header padding has-icons-right field has-addons is-marginless">
						<div class="col is-expanded is-fullwidth is-paddingless">
							<h5 class="has-text-grey-dark is-marginless">
								<b style="cursor:default;">3.  Why should organisations work towards the National Workplace Learning Certification?</b>
							</h5>
						</div>
						<span class="sgds-icon sgds-icon-plus is-size-4 bp-accordion-button"></span>
					</div><div id="accordion-body-2" class="col padding bp-accordion-body">
						<div class="bp-container is-full padding--top--lg padding--bottom" style="width: 100%">
<p>The new <a href="/certification/introduction/">National Workplace Learning Certification</a> aims to help organisations adopt and build up workplace learning practices and capabilities. Robust workplace learning processes and capabilities can enhance an organisation’s workforce performance, improve retention rates and help develop and sustain its core competencies for strong business performance and continued competitiveness.</p>

</div>
					</div><div class="col is-large bp-accordion-header padding has-icons-right field has-addons is-marginless">
						<div class="col is-expanded is-fullwidth is-paddingless">
							<h5 class="has-text-grey-dark is-marginless">
								<b style="cursor:default;">4.  Are there different categories or levels of award in this certification?</b>
							</h5>
						</div>
						<span class="sgds-icon sgds-icon-plus is-size-4 bp-accordion-button"></span>
					</div><div id="accordion-body-3" class="col padding bp-accordion-body">
						<div class="bp-container is-full padding--top--lg padding--bottom" style="width: 100%">
<p>Depending on organisation’s pervasiveness of workplace learning implementation, it can achieve one of the four levels of certifications:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Workplace Learning Organisation of Competence</li>
  <li>Workplace Learning Organisation of Competence (Silver)</li>
  <li>Workplace Learning Organisation of Competence (Gold)</li>
  <li>Workplace Learning Organisation of Excellence (Platinum)</li>
</ol>

<p>Depending on the organisations’ needs and strategies, organisations may aspire to attain one of the four different levels of certifications to develop and augment their competencies and capabilities.</p>

<p>The highest level of award is the Workplace Learning Organisation of Excellence (Platinum), which recognises organisations that have met the highest standards in workplace learning implementation, benchmarked to best-in-class workplace learning practices.</p>

</div>
					</div><div class="col is-large bp-accordion-header padding has-icons-right field has-addons is-marginless">
						<div class="col is-expanded is-fullwidth is-paddingless">
							<h5 class="has-text-grey-dark is-marginless">
								<b style="cursor:default;">5.  How are organisation assessed and awarded their certification levels?</b>
							</h5>
						</div>
						<span class="sgds-icon sgds-icon-plus is-size-4 bp-accordion-button"></span>
					</div><div id="accordion-body-4" class="col padding bp-accordion-body">
						<div class="bp-container is-full padding--top--lg padding--bottom" style="width: 100%">
<p>The assessment of the organisation, using the National Workplace Learning Framework, is based on a 600 point-scale. Organisations seeking to be certified will be awarded according to the following:</p>

<p><strong>1. Workplace Learning Organisation of Competence</strong> must score at least 300 points, with all component garnering at least 50 points each. 
The validity period for the certification is for 3 years.</p>

<p><strong>2. Workplace Learning Organisation of Competence (Silver)</strong> must fulfil at least 330 points, with all the components garnering at least 50 points and a score of at least 80 points in one of the six components. 
The validity period for the certification is for 4 years.</p>

<p><strong>3. Workplace Learning Organisation of Competence (Gold)</strong> must fulfil at least 390 points, with all the components garnering least 50 points and a score of at least 80 points each for any three components. 
The validity period for the certification is for 4 years.</p>

<p><strong>4. Workplace Learning Organisation of Excellence (Platinum)</strong> must fulfil at least 480 points, and a score of 80 points for each of the six components. 
The validity period for the certification is for 5 years.</p>

</div>
					</div><div class="col is-large bp-accordion-header padding has-icons-right field has-addons is-marginless">
						<div class="col is-expanded is-fullwidth is-paddingless">
							<h5 class="has-text-grey-dark is-marginless">
								<b style="cursor:default;">6.  How can organisations be prepared for the certification process?</b>
							</h5>
						</div>
						<span class="sgds-icon sgds-icon-plus is-size-4 bp-accordion-button"></span>
					</div><div id="accordion-body-5" class="col padding bp-accordion-body">
						<div class="bp-container is-full padding--top--lg padding--bottom" style="width: 100%">
<p>The <a href="/certification/introduction/">National Workplace Learning Certification</a> is developed with reference to the National Workplace Learning Framework. Hence, organisations embarking on the certification process are strongly encouraged to understand the <a href="/framework/national-workplace-learning-framework/">National Workplace Learning Framework</a>.</p>

<p>The framework will guide organisations in establishing best-in-class workplace learning practices that enhance employee performance, improve retention rates and maximise business performance. Organisations can also refer to the framework to identify and close gaps in their workplace learning processes and capabilities, and in their implementation of a sustainable workplace learning system.</p>

<p>NACE is also offering dedicated <a href="/about-nace/training-programmes/">training programmes</a> to individuals and organisations keen to deepen their understanding of the framework and build capabilities in implementing workplace learning. Organisations not embarking on the certification process can also participate in the training programmes and vice versa.</p>

</div>
					</div><div class="col is-large bp-accordion-header padding has-icons-right field has-addons is-marginless">
						<div class="col is-expanded is-fullwidth is-paddingless">
							<h5 class="has-text-grey-dark is-marginless">
								<b style="cursor:default;">7.  How can organisations assess that they are ready to be certified?</b>
							</h5>
						</div>
						<span class="sgds-icon sgds-icon-plus is-size-4 bp-accordion-button"></span>
					</div><div id="accordion-body-6" class="col padding bp-accordion-body">
						<div class="bp-container is-full padding--top--lg padding--bottom" style="width: 100%">
<p>An <a href="/certification/processes-self-evaluation/">online self-evaluation form</a> is available for interested organisations to assess their readiness to be certified.</p>

<p>NACE will review each organisation’s self-evaluation, and will provide them with the results of the self-evaluation, highlighting their strength(s) and area(s) for improvement. Organisations that have decided to embark on the certification process can then approach NACE for further assistance.</p>

<p>Please note that this is only applicable for organisations registered in Singapore.</p>

</div>
					</div><div class="col is-large bp-accordion-header padding has-icons-right field has-addons is-marginless">
						<div class="col is-expanded is-fullwidth is-paddingless">
							<h5 class="has-text-grey-dark is-marginless">
								<b style="cursor:default;">8.  What is the next step forward should an organisation request to be certified?</b>
							</h5>
						</div>
						<span class="sgds-icon sgds-icon-plus is-size-4 bp-accordion-button"></span>
					</div><div id="accordion-body-7" class="col padding bp-accordion-body">
						<div class="bp-container is-full padding--top--lg padding--bottom" style="width: 100%">
<p>For organisations that have decided to embark on the certification process, they can submit an <a href="/certification/application/">online application for the certification</a>.</p>

<p>Upon submission of the required documentation and payment of certification fees, NACE will work with the organisation on the certification process.</p>

<p>Please note that this is only applicable for organisations registered in Singapore.</p>

</div>
					</div><div class="col is-large bp-accordion-header padding has-icons-right field has-addons is-marginless">
						<div class="col is-expanded is-fullwidth is-paddingless">
							<h5 class="has-text-grey-dark is-marginless">
								<b style="cursor:default;">9.  How long will the certification process take?</b>
							</h5>
						</div>
						<span class="sgds-icon sgds-icon-plus is-size-4 bp-accordion-button"></span>
					</div><div id="accordion-body-8" class="col padding bp-accordion-body">
						<div class="bp-container is-full padding--top--lg padding--bottom" style="width: 100%">
<p>It is estimated to take about four months. However, this may vary depending on each organisation’s size and scale of operations.</p>

</div>
					</div><div class="col is-large bp-accordion-header padding has-icons-right field has-addons is-marginless">
						<div class="col is-expanded is-fullwidth is-paddingless">
							<h5 class="has-text-grey-dark is-marginless">
								<b style="cursor:default;">10.  Is there a deadline for organisations to apply for the National Workplace Learning Certification?</b>
							</h5>
						</div>
						<span class="sgds-icon sgds-icon-plus is-size-4 bp-accordion-button"></span>
					</div><div id="accordion-body-9" class="col padding bp-accordion-body">
						<div class="bp-container is-full padding--top--lg padding--bottom" style="width: 100%">
<p>No, there is no deadline. The National Workplace Learning Certification is open for applications throughout the year.</p>

</div>
					</div><div class="col is-large bp-accordion-header padding has-icons-right field has-addons is-marginless">
						<div class="col is-expanded is-fullwidth is-paddingless">
							<h5 class="has-text-grey-dark is-marginless">
								<b style="cursor:default;">11.  Since the certification has a validity period, do organisations need to pay if they want to renew their certification?</b>
							</h5>
						</div>
						<span class="sgds-icon sgds-icon-plus is-size-4 bp-accordion-button"></span>
					</div><div id="accordion-body-10" class="col padding bp-accordion-body">
						<div class="bp-container is-full padding--top--lg padding--bottom" style="width: 100%">
<p>Yes, organisations will have to pay a <a href="/certification/application/">certification fee</a> when they submit an application for certification. This applies to both renewal and new certifications.</p>

</div>
					</div><div class="col is-large bp-accordion-header padding has-icons-right field has-addons is-marginless">
						<div class="col is-expanded is-fullwidth is-paddingless">
							<h5 class="has-text-grey-dark is-marginless">
								<b style="cursor:default;">12. How soon can an organisation apply to obtain the next level of certification?</b>
							</h5>
						</div>
						<span class="sgds-icon sgds-icon-plus is-size-4 bp-accordion-button"></span>
					</div><div id="accordion-body-11" class="col padding bp-accordion-body">
						<div class="bp-container is-full padding--top--lg padding--bottom" style="width: 100%">
<p>Organisations can apply for re-certification after 2 years of achieving the previous level. <a href="/certification/application/">Certification costs</a> will apply accordingly.</p>

<p>Eg. ABC Company achieved the Workplace Learning Organisation of Competence (Silver) on 1 Oct 2021. If they aspire to achieve the Workplace Learning Organisation of Competence (Gold), they can only apply to be certified again from 1 Oct 2023 onwards.</p>

</div>
					</div><div class="col is-large bp-accordion-header padding has-icons-right field has-addons is-marginless">
						<div class="col is-expanded is-fullwidth is-paddingless">
							<h5 class="has-text-grey-dark is-marginless">
								<b style="cursor:default;">13. Why is there a need for organisations to pay to be certified? Is there any funding available for organisations to offset the certification cost?</b>
							</h5>
						</div>
						<span class="sgds-icon sgds-icon-plus is-size-4 bp-accordion-button"></span>
					</div><div id="accordion-body-12" class="col padding bp-accordion-body">
						<div class="bp-container is-full padding--top--lg padding--bottom" style="width: 100%">
<p>The certification fees are based on a cost recovery model to defray manpower costs incurred to conduct the assessment of organisations.</p>

</div>
					</div><div class="col is-large bp-accordion-header padding has-icons-right field has-addons is-marginless">
						<div class="col is-expanded is-fullwidth is-paddingless">
							<h5 class="has-text-grey-dark is-marginless">
								<b style="cursor:default;">14. Is it compulsory for organisations to undergo this certification process?</b>
							</h5>
						</div>
						<span class="sgds-icon sgds-icon-plus is-size-4 bp-accordion-button"></span>
					</div><div id="accordion-body-13" class="col padding bp-accordion-body">
						<div class="bp-container is-full padding--top--lg padding--bottom" style="width: 100%">
<p>It is not compulsory for organisations to undergo this certification. However, companies are encouraged to do so in order to identify and close gaps in their workplace learning processes and capabilities. This will help them enhance their workforce performance and improve retention rates.</p>

</div>
					</div><div class="col is-large bp-accordion-header padding has-icons-right field has-addons is-marginless">

								<b style="cursor:default;">15. Any other enquiries?</b>
							</h5>
