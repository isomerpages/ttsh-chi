<h2>About the Health and Social Change Academy (HSCA)</h2>
<p>The CHI Health and Social Change Academy (HSCA) is at the forefront of
transforming healthcare in Singapore. HSCA is developing the leadership
and technical capabilities needed to shift the focus of care from hospitals
to the community. By empowering residents and building new understanding
and capabilities in our care teams, the academy supports NHG’s vision for
healthier, more resilient communities.</p>
