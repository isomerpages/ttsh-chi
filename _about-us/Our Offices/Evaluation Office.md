<h1>Evaluation Office</h1>
<h3>Who We Are</h3>
<p>The Evaluation Office supports clinicians and healthcare teams to conduct
rigorous evaluations of healthcare innovations. We provide guidance, resources,
and expertise to ensure that new technologies, interventions, and care
models are thoroughly assessed for their impact on clinical/patient outcomes,
cost-effectiveness, and real-world implementation success.</p>
<p>Whether teams are piloting a new digital health tool, testing a care pathway
redesign, or implementing an innovative treatment approach, we support
the design of studies that generate robust evidence about what works, for
whom, and under what conditions. Our evaluations aim to inform critical
decisions about whether to adopt, scale, or modify healthcare innovations.</p>
<p>Our mission is to build evaluation capacity across the healthcare system,
enabling evidence-based decision-making that ensures only effective, feasible,
and cost-effective innovations are implemented at scale.</p>
